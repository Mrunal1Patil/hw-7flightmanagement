Frequently Asked Questions

1. How to Cancel or Modify a Flight?

To cancel or modify your flight, visit your account page, find your current booking, and choose "Modify" or "Cancel". Check the terms for any fees that may apply.

2. What are the Baggage Policies?

You can find detailed baggage policies on the "Baggage Rules" page. Each ticket has baggage allowances that vary by destination and airline.

3. What are the Check-In Procedures?

Online check-in is available 24 hours before your flight. You can check in via the app or website by entering your booking details.

4. How do I Contact Customer Support?

You can contact customer support via the "Contact" page or by emailing support@wingman.com. Response times may vary depending on the time of day.
